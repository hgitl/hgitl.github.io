ABOUT

Hanâ LBATH


I am a PhD candidate in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science at Inria Grenoble - Rhône-Alpes and Laboratoire Jean Kuntzman, in the Statify team (Grenoble, France). Under the supervision of Sophie Achard, I am working on spatio-temporal statistical models for robust inference of functional connectivity networks from time series data.

After two years in French preparatory classes, an intensive undergraduate program in Mathematics and Physics, I chose a multidisciplinary education at INSA Lyon at the interface between Computer Science, Mathematics and Biology. In 2020, I earned my Master's in Bioinformatics and Modeling Engineering from INSA Lyon. In parallel, I obtained my Master's in Computer Science at ENS Lyon where I also took courses on Complex Systems.
